Orbital AI Driven Design

Orbital ADD was created in Aug. 2020 as a website to carry a blog for Kevin Ritter. At the time he was still in his last semester of school. If you are thinking, but wait. The name evokes some kind of company dealing with Artificial Intelligence, well you're right to think that. Kevin planned the site from the beginning to eventually morph into a home for a showcase of projects that demonstrate his skill with AI. In particular his interest in genetic algorithms. The ultimate goal of which is the foundation of a consulting company that deals with one off projects of a small scale. The blog is simply a way to expand his web design skill set and will be only a small part of the eventual website's 'final form.' This site has its own public git hub repository where users can see the code behind it and log any issues that need attention.
